Nicole Cahoon CPA

Nicole Cahoon, CPA offers a full range of accounting and bookkeeping services to small and mid-sized businesses in Courtenay, Comox, Cumberland and all neighbouring towns in the Comox Valley. At Nicole Cahoon, CPA we are committed to provide a variety of services to meet the needs of our clients by fostering an environment that encourages personal and professional growth. We are dedicated to provide excellent customer service by focusing on the three underlying principles of professionalism, responsiveness and quality. We specialize in small to medium size businesses in the Comox Valley and surrounding areas.

- Comox Valley Accounting Services -

We provide constructive solutions for maximizing your company’s profitability.

  • Corporate Year Ends -Financial Statement Preparation
  • Corporate Tax Returns
  • Canadian Tax Returns for Individuals
  • Tax Planning
  • Estate Planning

- Business Consulting Services -

We can help your Comox valley business identify areas that affecting profitability and growth and we can develop solutions that are right for you.

  • Planning and Consulting
  • Starting a Business
  • Buying or Selling a Business
  • Mergers and Acquisitions

- Tax Services -

We can develop tax – minimization strategies for small and mid sized Comox Valley businesses as well and personal, to optimize your after – tax position.

  • Bookkeeping and data entry
  • Payroll services
  • Federal and Provincial Sales Tax (GST, HST, PST)
  • Computer Applications
